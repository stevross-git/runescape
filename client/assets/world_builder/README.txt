RuneScape World Builder Assets
==============================

This folder contains placeholder images and AI prompts for the world builder.

To create high-quality assets:
1. Check the 'prompts' folder for AI generation prompts
2. Use your preferred AI image generator (DALL-E, Midjourney, etc.)
3. Replace the placeholder PNG files with your generated images
4. Keep the same filenames and dimensions
